About Us

ERamen is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. ERamen has explored the world up to 50 countries. Every day, more than 8 million guests visit ERamen restaurants around the world. Our restaurant is famous for serving high quality, tasty and affordable food while maintaining the same taste, made from organic spices which are rich in Japanese taste while always following the taste.

Our History

ERamen is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura. Starting from a mother named Takara Sora found a distinctive taste of a ramen, in Tokyo, Japan. From our humble beginnings as a small food stall, we are proud to be one of the oldest and oldest ramen shops in the world.
